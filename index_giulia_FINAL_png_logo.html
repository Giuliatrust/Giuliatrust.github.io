Giulia Trust

Born from care, created by neurodivergence, for minds navigating a world not designed for them.

A journey from noise to clarity, from scattered pieces to structures that finally make sense.

Giulia Trust was created after Giulia, as dementia reshaped daily life and revealed how fragile dignity becomes inside complex systems. Founded by someone who lives neurodivergence, the Trust exists to support people who struggle not because they lack ability, but because the world around them was never designed with cognitive difference in mind.

Contact: info@giuliatrust.org
